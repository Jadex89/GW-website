Join sisters Amelia and Sarah on an uproarious adventure in "The Mad Vacuum Cleaner." Life at the Johnson household takes an unexpected turn when their trusty robot vacuum cleaner, newly purchased by their beleaguered Dad, goes mad! Chaos ensues as the mischievous machine creates mayhem in every corner of their home.

As the dynamic duo of Amelia and Sarah races against time to outsmart the wild Robo-Sweep, readers are in for a rollercoaster of laughs, surprises, and clever problem-solving. Can the sisters rein in the crazed contraption before their home becomes a whirlwind of wackiness?
